Given an integer B, please find out within 1-300, how many square of those integers, after changing into base B,
the values are palindrome.

Input:
B
Output
Square of the integer in B base, integer in B base. (They are palindrome)

Constraints
2≤B≤20,
when B>=10, use A, B, C...

Sample input
10
Sample output
1 1
2 4
3 9
11 121
22 484
26 676
101 10201
111 12321
121 14641
202 40804
212 44944
264 69696
