Given an integer n and m different prime numbers p1,p2,…,pm.

Find out how many integers are there in  1∼n which can be divided by at least one of p1,p2,…,pm

Input
 n  m
 m prime integers

Output
numbers that meets the requirement

constraints
1≤m≤16,
1≤n,pi≤10^9

Input sample:
10 2
2 3

Output sample:
7
