Given an integer N，find out all denominator <= N，value [0,1] simplified fraction, printout in ascending order.

For example, when N=5 all the fractions are:

01,15,14,13,25,12,35,23,34,45,11
Input:
N

Output
All the fractions in ascending order

constraints:
1≤N≤160

Input:
5
Output:
0/1
1/5

1/3
2/5
1/2
3/5
2/3
3/4
4/5
