Mike tries to cross NxN matrix from upper left corner to bottom right corner.

The total steps need to be no more than 2N−1.

He needs to pay for each step

what is the minimal cost.

Sample input
N
cost of each step

Sample output
minimal cost

constraints:
1≤N≤100

Sample input:
5
1  4  6  8  10
2  5  7  15 17
6  8  9  18 20
10 11 12 19 21
20 23 25 29 33

Sample output:
109
