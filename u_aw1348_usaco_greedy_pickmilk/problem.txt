Milk Daily needs
Each farmer can provide milk unit price
The most capacity each farmer can provide
What is the lowest cost for the company to meet the daily milk requirement.

Input
n, m, daily milk needs and farmers count
Pi, Ai farmers unit price and milk capacity

Output
output: lowest cost

Constraints:
0≤N≤2×10^6,
0≤M≤5000,
0≤Pi≤1000,
0≤Ai≤2×10^6

Sample input:
100 5
5 20
9 40
3 10
8 80
6 30
Sample output:
630
