Input
V means there are 1~V vitamin
cow daily needs for ith vitamin
G cow food type
each food contain vitamin

Output
S(how many type of food), ascending of the food number

Constraints
1≤V≤25,
1≤G≤15

4
100 200 300 400
3
50 50 50 50
200 300 200 300
900 150 389 399

Sample output:
2 1 3
