Given (a1,a2,…,aN)，we can get ascending subarray (ai1,ai2,…,aiK)，which 1≤i1<i2<…<iK≤N。

For example (1,7,3,5,9,4,8)，has ascending subarray like (1,7),(3,4,8)

The maximum sum are (1,3,5,9)

Find the maximum sum for ascending array

constraints:
1≤N≤1000

Sample input:
7
1 7 3 5 9 4 8
Sample output:
18
