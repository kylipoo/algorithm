Three bucket with capacity A,B,C liters.

A, B are initially empty，C is full.

Pouring among all the buckets, find out when A is empty, how much milk in C

Input
A,B,C。

Output
共一行，包含若干个整数，表示 C 桶中牛奶存量的所有可能情况，请将这些数字按升序排列。

Constraints:
1≤A,B,C≤20

Sample input1：
8 9 10
Sample output1：
1 2 8 9 10
Sample input2：
2 5 10
Sample output2：
5 6 7 8 9 10
