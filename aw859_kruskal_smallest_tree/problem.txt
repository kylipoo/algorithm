Given a no direction graph with n nodes and m edges. It might contain duplicate edges and self circle. The edge weight can be negative.

Find out the smallest weight sum of the tree made up by the n nodes. If it is impossible, print out "impossible".

constraints:
1≤n≤10^5 ,
1≤m≤2∗10^5,

Sample Input:
4 5
1 2 1
1 3 2
1 4 3
2 3 2
3 4 4

Sample output
6
