Hello Kitty walks through a field as a grid matrix, each grid intersection has some peanuts
She starts from upper left corner and end at right bottom corner.
She can only walk towards east or south
How many peanuts can she pick up the most.

Input
T means how many sets of data
R C rows and columns of the matrix

Output
max peanuts kitty can collect

Constraints
1≤T≤100,
1≤R,C≤100,
0≤M≤1000

Sample input:
2
2 2
1 1
3 4
2 3
2 3 4
1 6 5

Sample output:
8
16
