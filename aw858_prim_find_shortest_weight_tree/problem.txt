An one way graph has n nodes and m edges. the node number from 1 ~ n, the graph might have circle. and the edge can have negative weight

Find out the tree that generated with the smallest sum of weight. If it can not generate a tree, print "impossible"

constraints
1≤n≤500 ,
1≤m≤10^5,

Sample input:
4 5
1 2 1
1 3 2
1 4 3
2 3 2
3 4 4

Sample output:
6
